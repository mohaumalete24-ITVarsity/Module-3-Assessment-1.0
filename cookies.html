Steps

  1. Cream butter in a large mixing bowl;gradually add sugar, beating until light and fluffy. Add eggs, one at a time,beating well each addition. Add coffee, stirrig well.
  2. Combine flour, soda, cinnamon, and allspice in a large mixing bowl. Stir in raisins and pecans to coat well; add to creamed mixture.
  3. Drop dough by heaping teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto greased cookie sheets. Bake at 350 degrees for 8 minutes. Remove from cookie sheets, and cool on wire racks.
